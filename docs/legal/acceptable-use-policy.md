# Acceptable Use Policy

## 1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy sets minimum conduct rules for customer organizations and authorized users of Zemio.

## 2. Permitted Use

Zemio may be used only for legitimate internal workflows connected to expense reporting, review, reimbursement administration, and closely related organizational processes.

## 3. Prohibited Use

Users must not use Zemio:

- for unlawful, fraudulent, or deceptive conduct;
- to upload malware or harmful code;
- to attempt unauthorized access to data, accounts, or infrastructure;
- to overload or disrupt the service;
- to store content unrelated to the agreed use case;
- to infringe privacy, confidentiality, copyright, or other third-party rights;
- to process personal data in violation of applicable law or internal organizational authority.

## 4. Security Obligations

Users must:

- keep their Microsoft account secure;
- use the service only through authorized accounts;
- notify their organization and Zemio promptly of suspected misuse or unauthorized access.

## 5. Enforcement

Zemio may investigate suspected violations and may impose proportionate measures including warning, restriction, suspension, or termination for cause.
