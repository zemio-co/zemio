# Retention and Deletion Policy

This document describes the current target retention policy for Zemio. It is a contractual and operational draft and should be reviewed before launch.

## 1. General Principles

Zemio aims to:

- retain sensitive data only as long as necessary for operational and legal purposes;
- retain non-sensitive data only where there is a legitimate operational need or contractual reason;
- permit export and orderly deletion on termination;
- preserve data longer where legal retention obligations, disputes, or security investigations require it.

## 2. Target Retention Periods

### 2.1 Account Data

- retained while the user account exists;
- after account deletion, target 30-day deletion window, unless longer retention is required for legal, security, abuse-prevention, or contractual reasons.

### 2.2 Session and Authentication Logs

- target retention: 90 days.

### 2.3 Invitations

- target retention: 180 days after expiry.

### 2.4 Reports and Attachments

- target retention: for the contract term plus up to 12 months after termination;
- earlier deletion may occur if requested by the customer and no legal obligation, dispute, or legal hold prevents deletion.

### 2.5 Banking Details

- retained while required for active report workflows;
- deleted upon account deletion or earlier deletion request where technically and legally possible;
- if banking details remain necessary for retained reports or legal obligations, deletion may be deferred accordingly.

### 2.6 Backups

- target retention: 30 days.

### 2.7 Monitoring and Operational Logs

- target retention: 30 days, unless longer retention is required for incident analysis or legal defense.

### 2.8 Billing and Accounting Records

- retained for the period required by applicable tax and commercial law.

## 3. Deletion Triggers

Deletion may be triggered by:

- account deletion by the user or customer administrator;
- customer request after termination;
- expiry of retention periods;
- removal of data no longer necessary for the service purpose.

## 4. Export Before Deletion

Customers should be given a reasonable opportunity to export their data before final deletion after contract end.

## 5. Exceptions

Data may be retained beyond the target periods where reasonably necessary for:

- compliance with legal obligations;
- prevention or investigation of abuse or security incidents;
- establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims;
- enforcement of contractual rights;
- integrity of limited backup cycles.

## 6. Review

This retention policy may be updated where required by law, operational needs, or changes to Zemio's service design.
